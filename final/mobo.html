Socket Compatibility (CPU Support)

  • The socket must match your CPU.
  • Intel: LGA 1700 (12th/13th/14th gen)
  • AMD: AM4 (Ryzen 1000–5000), AM5 (Ryzen 7000+)
  • Always check CPU support on the motherboard manufacturer’s website.

Form Factor (Size)

  • ATX: Full size, most expansion slots.
  • Micro-ATX (mATX): Smaller, fewer slots, still versatile.
  • Mini-ITX: Very compact, minimal expandability (for small builds).

Key Gaming Features

  • PCIe x16 slot for your GPU (latest: PCIe 4.0 or 5.0).
  • M.2 NVMe slots for fast SSDs (look for heatsinks included).
  • High-quality VRMs for CPU stability (important if overclocking).
  • RAM Support – Fast DDR4 or DDR5 support depending on CPU/gen.
  • USB Ports – USB 3.2 Gen2 or USB-C preferred.
  • BIOS Flashback – Useful for upgrading CPUs without a supported CPU.
