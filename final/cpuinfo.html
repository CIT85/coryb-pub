Choosing the Right CPU for Your Gaming PC

When building a gaming PC, selecting the right CPU is crucial for achieving the performance you want without overspending. Budget CPUs are ideal for casual gamers, light multitasking, or entry-level builds where value is key. On the other hand, performance CPUs are designed for enthusiasts who demand high frame rates, smooth multitasking, and power for streaming, editing, or running demanding applications. Below you'll find two categorized lists of CPUs to help you make an informed decision based on your goals and budget.

CPU Lists

Intel Core i3-12100F

AMD Ryzen 5 5500
